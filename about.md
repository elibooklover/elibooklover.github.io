## Hey, I'm Hoyeol.

I'm an AI Quality Assurance Engineer based in Dallas, Texas. My work sits at the intersection of data engineering and AI evaluation — basically, I make sure AI systems actually work the way they're supposed to, from tiny edge devices all the way up to large-scale cloud infrastructure.

Right now I'm at Samsung Electronics America, and before that I spent time at AWS working on AI data after finishing my PhD.

---

### What I work on

**Speech & Language AI** — ASR evaluation, Inverse Text Normalization, and wrangling multilingual datasets. If it involves making a machine understand human speech accurately, I've probably touched it.

**Generative AI & LLMs** — prompt analysis, model response validation, and making sure AI behaves responsibly across Guardrails, RLHF, and RAG setups.

**ML Infrastructure & Automation** — building Python pipelines for automated testing across iOS and Android, plus analytics tools to make sense of benchmark results at scale.

---

### What I'm curious about

I'm particularly interested in making language models more efficient for non-English languages — specifically Korean LLM tokenization and token-pruning. There's a lot of room to improve how models handle Korean, and I find that problem genuinely fun to dig into.
